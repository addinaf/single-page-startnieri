startnieri:
we help the stranieri to start!

startnieri is an all-in-one information source for the obligatory paperwork designed for a foreigner residing in Italy

concept design by Addina Faizati

startnieri is an all in one app service where the foreigners can collect the information regarding the obligatory documents and paperwork that they need as a foreigner living in Italy. The app provided the information and the aims of the documents, and to keep them in track with the process.
startnieri aim to be a complete guide for the foreigner's obligatory paperwork that they need to do in order to make it easier and faster to keep in track with the bureaucracy process. The seamless experience of a foreigner during the process eventually will make the bureaucracy more efficient and faster for the staffs in charge and the government as well in the long term period. To gain the trust and cut the cost of the time and energy wasted from the efficiency.
